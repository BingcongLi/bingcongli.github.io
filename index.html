About me

I received the doctoral degree from the University of Minnesota under the supervision of Prof. Georgios B. Giannakis. Before that, I received BS degree in Electronic Engineering from Fudan University advised by Prof. Xin Wang.

My research interests lie in the theoretical foundation of optimization, with an emphasis on first order methods. I hope to gain inspiring insights from in-depth analysis of existing methods, and leverage them to develop efficient and scalable optimization solvers.
